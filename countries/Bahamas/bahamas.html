The Bahamas

The Bahamas is regarded as part of the Caribbean, even though the archipelago of over 700 islands and cays, strung together with deep ocean channels and shallow banks, is actually located in the Atlantic and not the Caribbean Sea. For most visitors, The Bahamas' number one attraction is beaches. There is something really special about the powder-soft, limestone-white sands you find here, and the shallow shorelines and kaleidoscope of blues connecting the sea and sky in a seamless horizon(Wikipedia)
